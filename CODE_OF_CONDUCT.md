# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Civia a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, public or private — including but not limited to insults,
  derogatory comments, personal attacks, or political attacks targeting
  individuals
- Trolling, deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

Civia.ro nu promovează niciun partid politic, nicio poziționare
ideologică. Discuțiile pe issue-uri și PR-uri trebuie să rămână
focusate pe cod, date și impact civic — nu pe afilieri politice.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to
maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct,
and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.
