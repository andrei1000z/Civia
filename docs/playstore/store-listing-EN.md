🇷🇴 Civia — Romania's civic platform

Civia turns civic reporting from bureaucratic torture into 90 seconds of action. Take a photo of the problem, write 2-3 lines, and AI generates the formal text + sends it to the right authority. By Romanian law (OG 27/2002), city hall must respond within 30 days — Civia tracks everything.

🎯 WHAT CIVIA DOES
• Sends official reports to city halls, prefectures, road authorities, local police
• AI writes formal legal text (compliant with OG 27/2002)
• Auto-detects competent authority from GPS location
• 1-tap camera for instant street reporting
• Push notifications when authorities respond
• Tracks status: registered, in progress, resolved
• Works offline — reports send when signal returns

🤖 AI INSIDE
• Llama 4 Scout Vision — detects problem type from photo
• Llama 3.3 70B — formulates text in Romanian legal language
• Anti-spam and anti-fraud for authority replies
• Auto-summarization of civic news

🏛️ NATIONAL COVERAGE
• 42 counties + 6 Bucharest sectors
• 220+ municipalities and cities
• 1500+ indexed authorities (city halls, prefectures, agencies)
• Official OpenStreetMap data for maps

📊 CIVIC FEATURES
• Reports — submit problems to authorities
• Petitions — sign collective civic causes
• Protests — public civic events calendar
• News — daily aggregation from 30+ Romanian sources
• Guides — how to contest fines, OG 27/2002 explained, Law 544
• Outages — water, gas, electricity, scheduled works
• City hall ranking — who responds best

🔒 PRIVACY & GDPR
• EU servers (Frankfurt)
• Data encrypted at rest + in transit (HTTPS + TLS 1.3)
• GDPR-compliant + Romanian Constitution
• Your name appears ONLY in the official email — NOT on the public page (default)
• Delete account anytime from profile

🆓 FREE FOREVER
Civia is 100% free. We don't sell personal data. No money from city halls, parties, or government. Open-source on GitHub (andrei1000z/Civia). Mission: democratize access to civic information in Romania.

📲 HOW IT WORKS IN 3 STEPS
1. Take a photo of the problem (pothole, illegal parking, garbage)
2. Write 2-3 lines — AI rewrites formally
3. Tap „Send" — email goes officially to city hall

⚖️ LEGAL BASIS

🆘 IF CITY HALL DOESN'T RESPOND
Civia automatically generates a complaint to the People's Advocate (Avocatul Poporului) 30 days after submission. Free, public, effective.
